    Scra/mbled eggs

    Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Her is a recipe for deliciously rich Scrambled eggs.

    Ingredients

    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 2tbsp creem

    Method

    1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
    2. Gently mix t/he eggs and and cream in a bowl
    3. Once the butter has melted add the eggs and cream
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are trying to make an omlette)
    5. When the eggs are moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until severed)
