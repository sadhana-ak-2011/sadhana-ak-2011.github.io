    Film-Making Terms

    Arc Shot
    Interlude
    Proluge

    Arc Shot

    A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

    Interlude

    A brief, interventing film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

    Proluge

    A speech,preface,introduction, or a brief scene preceding the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilouge
