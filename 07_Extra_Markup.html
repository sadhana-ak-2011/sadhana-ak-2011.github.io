Olafur Elissons

Olafur Elisson was born in Conpenhagen,Denmark in 1967 to Icelandic parents.

He is known for sculptures and large-scale installation art emplying elemental materials such as light, water, air temperature to enhance the viewer's experience.

Water and air. So very commonplace are these substances, are hardly attract attention - and they vouchsafe our very existence.

Every time i view the sea I feel a calming sense of security, as if visting my ancestral home; I embark on a voyage of , seeing.

Mystery of mysteries, water and air are right there before us in the sea.
